Get To Know Us

Gallery Social Media

We are PT Jawa Tengah Lahan Andalan and the subsidiary of Mugan Group. PT Jawa Tengah Lahan Andalan is the developer and superintendent of industrial estate. We manage Jatengland Industrial Park Sayung (JIPS), with 300 hectares business and industrial zone. We have arranged the steps of development into 3 (three) phases, which are Phase I: 110 hectares; Phase II: 120 hectares; Phase III: 70 hectares. JIPS design is officially acknowledged as the industrial zone on Demak Spatial Planning (RTRW – Rencana Tata Ruang Wilayah). Furthermore, JIPS has a strategic location because it is located in the path of national Pantura access, that is Semarang – Surabaya and nearby the railway station, international seaport and airport.

PT Jawa Tengah Lahan Andalan always makes improvements to achieve the goal and support the government. Expectedly, JIPS could be a competitive and sustainable industrial estate. Similarly, JIPS could also create the atmosphere in a global business competition as the industries inside would operate their business field by linking and networking with communities, government, public and some others. Still, the existence of industrial estate shall facilitate the investors and industries to expand their business.

Conceptual Prospects

We accommodate your business concerns through our concept, that is “one” integrated Cyber Techno Eco – concept. We dedicate our excellent service so that you could get all your needs in one zone, as Integrated Access Control System, Full Area Hotspot, Independent Green Power Supply, 24 Hours Security, Customer Service Unit, Legal Support Service, Human Resource, etc. Various facilities are also available to support our tenants, including WWTP (Waste Water Treatment Plan), smat IT by PT Telkom (Persero), electricity by PT PLN (Persero), water management system, green open spaces, fire fighting facilities, fitness centers, places of worship and food courts.

As you are our priority, we commit to serve heartily. We give the good opportunity in our special offers. Business plots, warehousing, commercial units (ruko), and some others in our industrial estate could be your ultimate choice for your investment.
