Warmest Greetings

In the globalization era, everyone is required to have the power of struggle. The improvement must be in balance with the quality of human resources so each could face the challenge and circumstances. Discipline and hardworking are extremely needed for the success of person, team and company..

PT Jawa Tengah Lahan Andalan, as a subsidiary of Mugan Group, has strongly committed to promote regional prospects, in Demak Regency, Central Java Province. This commitment is actualized in Jatengland Industrial Park Sayung (JIPS). By the existence of this industrial estate, Central Java Province is expected to be a pioneer in Indonesia and ‘go international.

I would like to express my gratitude to our great team of PT Jawa Tengah Lahan Andalan who has well enforced Jatengland Industrial Park Sayung (JIPS) from planning steps until its development. In this case, a solid cooperation is very important for the achievement of success. Thank you for all institutions, communities and related parties who have supported us in the development of Jatengland Industrial Park Sayung (JIPS).

More, we manage to give the best for our tenants and relations because we emphasize an excellent service. Confidently, we wish PT Jawa Tengah Lahan Andalan could be an advanced industrial estate and it could go forward to internationally promote Demak, Central Java Province, and the Indonesian Republic.

Once again, thank you, and wish you have a blessed day
